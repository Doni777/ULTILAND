FAQ

Questions

ULTILAND is a Web3-native RWA protocol that tokenizes art, cultural IP, antiques, and creative assets. Founded in 2023, it bridges the $6.2 trillion cultural economy with DeFi using dual-token ($ARTX / miniARTX), AI agents, and ZK-powered IP tools. DeArt meets DeFi.
$ARTX is the sovereign governance and utility token of ULTILAND. It's used for DAO voting, RWA settlement, staking rewards, and ecosystem participation. Max supply is capped at 280M. Listed on Binance Alpha, MEXC, and BingX as ARTXUSDT trading pair on BNB Chain (BEP-20).
miniARTX is an escrowed behavioral certificate that tracks your contribution and engagement on the platform. It doesn't freely circulate — it converts into $ARTX through controlled unlocking mechanisms tied to liquidity participation. It's the sole source of new ARTX emissions.
The snapshot was taken Nov 19, 2025, with airdrop rolling out in batches from Nov 28. Connect your wallet via the Claim section and verify eligibility. Binance Alpha users with 255+ Alpha Points received 200 ARTX. EMQL holders and Genesis Raffle winners receive higher allocations.
Assets go through evaluation, certification, and verification via the Special Asset Entity (SAE), which ensures physical assets are secure and risk-isolated. The platform then issues an ARToken — a hybrid NFT/fungible token — with the original asset's provenance and value recorded immutably on-chain.
ULTILAND was founded in 2023 by a team from art institutions, blockchain engineering, Web3 product development, and traditional finance across Hong Kong, Mainland China, North America, and Southeast Asia. Partners include Sparkle AI, StarAI, BaumzTrade, and TradeTide AI.
